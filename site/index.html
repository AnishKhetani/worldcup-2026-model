How it works

A Dixon-Coles Poisson goal model that fits a separate attack and defence rating for every team by weighted maximum-likelihood on ~17k international matches (recent games and major tournaments weighted more), with a two-sided home-advantage term and the standard low-scoring-draw correction. Knockout progression (extra time / penalties) uses a favorite-conversion calibrated on 92 years of World Cup knockout history — not a coin-flip shootout. Each match is predicted walk-forward from only earlier games, so there is no lookahead. Full method: SPEC.md.
